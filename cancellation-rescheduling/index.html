Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Effective date: December 2025
Last updated: December 2025

We understand that life happens and plans sometimes change. This Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy explains how to change or cancel an appointment with FlatRate Furniture Assembly & Tech Concierge.

1. How to Cancel or Reschedule

The easiest way to cancel or reschedule is to call or text us as soon as you know you need to make a change:

Phone/Text: 347-978-8672

2. Standard Policy (Furniture Assembly & Tech Concierge)

Unless we agree otherwise in writing, the following policy applies to both furniture assembly and tech concierge visits:

  • More than 24 hours before your appointment: You can cancel or reschedule at no charge.
  • Within 24 hours of your appointment: We may charge a late cancellation fee (usually a portion of the visit fee) to cover reserved time.
  • No-show or unable to access the property: If we arrive and cannot access the location or no adult (18+) is present, we may charge a service call or no-show fee.

The exact fee (if any) will be explained at the time of booking and/or in your confirmation message.

3. Weather, Safety, and Other Exceptions

We may need to reschedule an appointment for reasons such as:

  • Severe weather or unsafe travel conditions
  • Illness, emergency, or other situations affecting our ability to provide safe service
  • Unforeseen issues at the service location that make it unsafe or unreasonable to perform the work

In these situations, we will make a good-faith effort to contact you as soon as possible and agree on a new date and time. Fees are generally not charged when we initiate a reschedule for safety or weather reasons.

4. Deposit and Pre-Payment Policy (If Applicable)

For larger or more complex jobs, we may require a deposit or partial pre-payment to hold your appointment. If a deposit applies, we will explain:

  • The amount of the deposit
  • How it will be applied to your final bill
  • Under what conditions it may be non-refundable or partially refundable

5. Changes in Scope of Work

If the work on site is significantly different from what was described when booking (for example, extra furniture items or additional tech issues), we may:

  • Adjust the price to reflect the actual work needed, and/or
  • Schedule a follow-up visit if there is not enough time in the original time slot
