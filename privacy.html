This Privacy Policy explains how FlatRate Furniture Assembly & Tech Concierge (“FlatRate”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website (www.flatratefurnitureassembly.com), contact us, or use our in-home furniture assembly and tech concierge / senior tech support services in the Bronx, NY area.

1.1 Information you provide directly

When you contact us or book a service, we may collect:

  • Name

  • Service address or general location (e.g. “Riverdale 10463”)

Like many websites, we may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) which collect information such as:

  • IP address and approximate location

This information is generally used in aggregate form to understand how visitors use our website and to improve our content and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Responding to your inquiries and service requests

  • Improving our website, services, and customer experience
  • Keeping basic records for business, accounting, and tax purposes

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in limited situations, including:

When you provide a phone number, you agree that we may use it to call or text you about:

We keep your information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, such as:
